EDGI: Enhancing Access to Environmental Violation Data

Overview

Team

1 PM, 2 TLs, 2 UX designers, 8 developers

Role

UX Designer

Timeline

Fall 2023 (3 months)

Client

EDGI (Environmental Data Governance Initiative) is an action-oriented collaborative watchdog that aims to identify the ways the EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) neglects various communities throughout the United States.

Goal

Create an interactive map web app to display county-based Environmental Protection Act violation data for educators, students, journalists, and informed members of the public to easily digest.
